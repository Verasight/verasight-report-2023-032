# Methodology

Methodology summary for MPSA 2023 Omnibus Survey #2023-032.

## Survey

- Sponsor: Verasight
- Field dates: 2023-04-19 to 2023-04-27
- Population: United States adults who completed both waves of the survey
- Mode: online panel (Verasight Community)
- Questions: 44
- Base size: N=1000.

## Weighting

- Weight variable: weight
- CPS variables: age, race/ethnicity, sex, income, education, region, metropolitan status
- Partisanship source: 2020 presidential vote and partisan identification population benchmarks (specific source not named in methods PDF)
